Welcome to Ever Increasing Grace - Candelaria!

Step into a vibrant community where believers are dedicated to sharing the life-changing message of love, grace, and faith. Our church is a united family where diverse individuals celebrate, learn, and flourish on their spiritual journey. Rooted in the teachings of Jesus Christ, we infuse boundless love and endless grace into every facet of church life through worship, sermons, and Bible studies, empowering members to navigate the complexities of the modern world.

Our strength lies in fostering an all-embracing environment that nurtures relationships and fosters genuine connections. Whether experienced or new to spirituality, Ever Increasing Grace is where you belong, grow, and thrive. With diverse ministries, engaging events, and impactful outreach, we spread positivity in our local community and beyond. Join us on this remarkable journey of faith, growth, and service, illuminating the world with compassion and hope.
