What We Do

  • Physical Services

    Experience the warmth of fellowship as we gather for physical services at our local church in Candelaria, Quezon. Join us in person to worship, connect, and grow together in the grace and teachings of Jesus Christ.

  • Online Live Services

    Join us for inspiring online live services on our Facebook page, where we bring the message of love, grace, and faith right to your screen. Experience worship, engaging sermons, and a community of believers united in their journey of faith.

  • Feeding Program for Kids

    We're passionate about making a difference. Our Kids Feeding and Outreach Program is a testament to our commitment to serving our community. Through this initiative, we provide nourishing meals to children and extend support to those in need, embodying the love and compassion of Christ.

  • Youth Ministry

    Calling all youth! Our vibrant Youth Ministry offers a space for growth, friendship, and fun. Join our exciting events and fellowships designed to help you navigate life's challenges while building a strong foundation of faith and community.

  • Men and Women's Ministry

    Nurturing fathers and mothers in the ministry, our dedicated Men's and Women's Ministries provide support, mentorship, and fellowship. As spiritual leaders, husbands, wives, and parents, we come together to strengthen our faith, families, and community bonds.
